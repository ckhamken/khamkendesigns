Why Work With Us

Your trusted engineering design and machining resource

Khamken Designs, LLC is a mechanical design and CNC machining service dedicated to transitioning ideas and requirements into value-driven products and solutions. From concept to machining, our goal is to ensure that customers' expectations are exceeded by providing comprehensive engineering design, low volume / high mix CNC machining, and assembly services at a high level.

We build upon mechanical design expertise spanning more than 10-years in industrial automation, including automotive assembly and testing (gas and electric), medical device assembly and testing, automated packaging, and robotic material handling. This is complemented by over 30-years of combined manufacturing experience supporting military, defense, and aerospace programs.

  • Honest Commitments
    We don’t overpromise. When we commit to a job, it’s because we’re confident it can be executed correctly.
  • Precision at Every Step
    Quality is built into our process — from planning and programming through machining and final inspection.
  • Clear, Proactive Communication
    If something changes, you’ll hear from us early — along with a clear plan forward.
  • Engineered Solutions
    From concept to production, we can assist with solving design challenges, recommending the right materials, and providing a second set of eyes ensuring every part is optimized for machining.

When Things Don’t Go Perfectly
Challenges are a reality of manufacturing, especially when exploring the "new". The difference is a disciplined response—fast communication, clear accountability, and collaborative resolution.

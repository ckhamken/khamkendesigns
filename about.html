Why Work With Us

Your trusted engineering design and machining resource

Khamken Designs, LLC is a mechanical design and CNC machining service dedicated to transitioning ideas and requirements into value-driven products and solutions. From concept to machining, our goal is to ensure that customers' expectations are exceeded by providing comprehensive engineering design, low volume / high mix CNC machining, and assembly services at a high level.

We build upon mechanical design expertise spanning more than 15-years in industrial automation, including automotive assembly and testing (gas and electric), medical device assembly and testing, automated packaging, and robotic material handling. This is complemented by over 30-years of combined manufacturing experience supporting military, defense, and aerospace programs.

Honest Commitments

We don’t overpromise. When we commit to a job, it’s because we’re confident it can be executed correctly.

Precision at Every Step

Quality is built into every stage — from first article verification through final inspection — so parts arrive to print, every time.

Clear, Proactive Communication

If a problem occurs, you'll hear from us first — with a clear explanation and a path forward, not a surprise at delivery.

Engineered Solutions

Engineering and machining under one roof — so design challenges get solved before they become production problems.

When Things Don’t Go Perfectly
Manufacturing isn't perfect — and anyone who says otherwise hasn't done enough of it. Problems happen. What we can control is how quickly we communicate, how honestly we own them, and how effectively we resolve them — together.

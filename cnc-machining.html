CNC Machining

Consistent precision and quality at any scale

Precision CNC machining centered on accuracy, consistency, and efficiency. Specializing in low-volume, high-mix, with the flexibility to support higher-volume production — maintaining the same high standards of quality at any scale.

  • Precision CNC machining solutions centered on accuracy, consistency, and efficiency.
    Every part is produced with careful attention to detail, process control, and repeatability to ensure reliable results from first article through final delivery.
  • Scalable Production Support
    While optimized for low-volume, high-mix work, our machining capabilities are flexible enough to support higher-volume production when required. Processes are structured to scale efficiently while maintaining consistent quality and dimensional control.
  • Integrated with Engineering & Prototyping
    Close alignment with mechanical engineering and prototyping services ensures designs are machined as intended. This integration reduces risk, shortens development cycles, and delivers production-ready parts faster.
  • Quality at Every Scale
    High standards are applied regardless of quantity. From one-off prototypes to repeat production runs, quality is factored into every step — planning, machining, inspection, and final verification — to ensure parts meet exact requirements.

Mechanical Assembly

Streamline your project by having parts machined and assembled in one place. With proven experience in machine and product assembly, components are machined accurately and efficiently, ensuring proper fit, function, and performance through careful, detail-oriented attention.

  • Complete system or sub-module assembly
  • Custom tooling assembly
  • Selection, torquing, thread-locking, and tamper-proofing of fasteners
  • Press-fitting bearings, bushings, dowel pins, etc.
  • Installation of specialty hardware including Heli-coils®, Keenserts®, etc.

Quality Inspection

All parts are produced and inspected in accordance with documented processes to verify conformity to specified requirements and dimensional tolerances. Calibrated inspection equipment and controlled measurement practices are used to ensure accuracy and consistency prior to shipment.

  • Calipers, micrometers, height gauge
  • Gage blocks, pin gauges, thread plug gages
  • Surface granite plate
  • Renishaw on-machine probe for in process verification

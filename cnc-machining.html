CNC Machining

Consistent precision and quality at any scale

Precision machining for complex, low-volume work — with the engineering background to get it right the first time.

  • Low-volume, high-mix specialists — with the flexibility to scale.
    Our sweet spot is complex, low-quantity work where attention to detail matters most. That said, processes are structured to support higher-volume repeat runs when required, without sacrificing dimensional control or delivery consistency.
  • Integrated from design through delivery.
    With in-house engineering and CNC machining under one roof, designs are machined exactly as functionally intended. This eliminates the back-and-forth between design and shop, reduces risk on first articles, and compresses lead times from concept to finished part.
  • Quality built into every step — not inspected in at the end.
    Parts are produced and verified against documented processes using calibrated inspection equipment. From first article through final shipment, dimensional conformance is confirmed at each stage — not assumed.

4th-axis Benefits
  • Multi-face milling
    Machine all faces in a single setup — no re-fixturing.
  • Rotational indexing
    Bolt patterns, radial holes, and angular features to 0.001° resolution.
  • Shaft & hub work
    Keyways, cross-holes, and turned features on parts up to 8-in (203-mm) diameter.
  • Helical contouring
    Continuous A-axis interpolation for threads, cams, and spiral features.

    Eliminate secondary operations and reduce part handling errors.

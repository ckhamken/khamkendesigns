Why Work With Us?

Khamken Designs, LLC is a mechanical design and CNC machining service dedicated to transitioning ideas and requirements into value-driven products and solutions. From concept to machining, our goal is to ensure that customers' expectations are exceeded by providing comprehensive engineering design, low volume / high mix CNC machining, and assembly services at a high level.

We build upon mechanical design expertise spanning more than 10-years in industrial automation, including automotive assembly and testing (gas and electric), medical device assembly and testing, automated packaging, and robotic material handling. This is complemented by over 30-years of combined manufacturing experience supporting military, defense, and aerospace programs.
